Fight the Blight! TD is a mobile 3D tower defense game created in Unity using C#. Defend your path from the monster's attack by placing powerful attack towers. Unique hero characters turbo charge your attacks with powerful effects. Unlock more levels as you progress through the game.

Google Play Store integration via In-App Purchases allow for purchasing unique characters, powerful unit upgrades, and more in-game currency for the player to spend.

Google Admob integration allows for ad-supported play using several monetization paradigms: small inobtrusive banner ads on levels, skippable interstitial ads between levels, as well as rewarded interstitials to unlock free plays.

Check it out in the Google Play Store today!

Karmic Adventures is a 2D RPG game in development. It was written in C# using the Unity game engine. Gameplay and mechanics are being developed and prototyped.

The RPG will feature open-world style gameplay and will have a rich main storyline to play through as well as a wide variety of side quests to flesh out the world and provide hundreds of hours of non-sequential gameplay.
