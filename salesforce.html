Apex

The Expense Report Lightning Web Component is composed from several components:

  • Employee Panel
  • Expense Report Panel
  • Expense Item Panel
  • Employee Tile
  • Expense Report Tile
  • Expense Item Tile

Each panel queries a custom sObject and pulls in the appropriate records. The custom Apex controller respects sharing rules and SOQL queries are run with security enforced to take into account permissions and field level security.

The tiles display basic information from each associated record. The magnifying glass will display the related records associated with the tile's record in the appropriate panel. The up arrow icon will open the record in another tab.
